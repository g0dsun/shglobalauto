Why Korean Cars Dominate in Kazakhstan

Established Market

Korean brands hold over 30% of Kazakhstan's auto market. Hyundai and Kia have official dealerships nationwide, ensuring strong parts supply and resale value.

Superior Condition

Korea's mandatory biennial inspections ensure all exported vehicles are well-maintained. Average mileage is lower than European or Japanese counterparts.

Advanced Features

Korean-market vehicles come loaded with features: heated seats, smart cruise, advanced safety systems — often standard equipment rather than costly options.

Climate Compatibility

Korean and Kazakh climates share extreme temperature ranges. Vehicles are engineered for -30°C winters and +40°C summers — perfect for Kazakhstan's continental climate.

Key Regulations

  • Left-hand drive only — RHD vehicles cannot be registered
  • Euro 4+ emission standard required
  • SBKTS certificate required for vehicle registration
  • VAT: 12% on customs value + duty
  • Recycling fee (utilization charge) — based on engine size and vehicle age
  • First registration tax applies upon registration in Kazakhstan

Required Documents

  • Bill of Lading (B/L)
  • Commercial invoice
  • Korean export certificate
  • Vehicle registration copy
  • Performance inspection report
  • SBKTS certificate (obtained in Kazakhstan)
  • Passport / ID of importer

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the customs duty for importing a used car to Kazakhstan?

Kazakhstan follows EAEU tariffs. For vehicles 3-5 years old, rates are EUR 1.5-3.6 per cc. For vehicles over 5 years, EUR 2.5-5.7 per cc. Additional VAT (12%) and recycling fee apply.

How long does shipping take from Korea to Almaty?

Via the Vladivostok route: 2-3 weeks total. Sea freight takes 5-7 days, rail/truck transport to Almaty takes 7-10 days.

Is there a vehicle age limit for imports to Kazakhstan?

No strict age ban, but vehicles over 7 years face significantly higher duties and recycling fees. The sweet spot is 3-5 year old vehicles for the best value.

What is the SBKTS certificate?

SBKTS is the EAEU vehicle safety certificate. It confirms the vehicle meets technical regulations. Obtained at accredited testing centers in Kazakhstan after the vehicle arrives.

Can I drive a Korean car in Kazakhstan without modification?

Yes. Korean vehicles are left-hand drive like Kazakhstan. No steering conversion needed. You only need to pass technical inspection and get SBKTS.

What is the recycling fee in Kazakhstan?

The recycling fee is based on engine size and vehicle age. For passenger cars, the base is approximately 50 MCI (around 185,000 KZT in 2026), with multipliers up to 80x for older, larger-engine vehicles.

Does SH GLOBAL deliver to Astana?

Yes. Vehicles arrive in Almaty first, with onward transport to Astana arranged. Total additional transit time to Astana is 1-2 days.
