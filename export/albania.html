Import Regulations

Importing a Car to Albania

Customs Duties & Taxes

Albania applies customs duties and VAT on all imported vehicles. The total cost depends on the vehicle's age, engine size, and declared value:

Fee TypeRateNotes
Import Customs Duty15-20%Based on CIF value (cost + insurance + freight)
VAT20%Applied on CIF value + customs duty
Registration TaxVariesBased on engine size and vehicle age
Carbon/Environmental TaxVariesHigher for older vehicles, lower for Euro 5/6

Example: For a vehicle with $10,000 CIF value — customs duty ~$1,750 (17.5%) + VAT ~$2,350 (20% on $11,750) = approximately $4,100 in taxes, plus registration and environmental fees.

Key Regulations

  • Left-hand drive (LHD) only — right-hand drive vehicles are not permitted
  • Euro 4+ emissions recommended — vehicles meeting Euro 4 or higher face lower environmental taxes
  • No strict age limit — but older vehicles attract significantly higher taxes and environmental fees
  • VAT: 20% applied on customs value + duty
  • Currency: Albanian Lek (ALL) — all duties payable in local currency
  • Technical inspection required before registration at authorized centers

Required Documents

  • Bill of Lading (B/L)
  • Commercial invoice with vehicle details and CIF value
  • Korean export certificate
  • Vehicle registration copy
  • Performance inspection report
  • Certificate of origin
  • Emission compliance certificate
  • Insurance documentation

About Durres Port

Durres is Albania's largest and most important port, located just 33 km from Tirana. It handles the majority of Albania's vehicle imports and has well-established customs clearance procedures. Local customs brokers are available to assist with the clearance process.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the total cost of importing a used car to Albania?

The total import cost includes customs duty (15-20%), VAT (20% on CIF + duty), registration tax, and environmental fees. As a rough estimate, expect to pay 35-45% of the vehicle's CIF value in total taxes and fees. SH GLOBAL provides detailed cost estimates for each vehicle before purchase.

How long does shipping take from Korea to Durres?

RoRo shipping from Busan or Incheon to Durres port takes approximately 35-45 days, depending on the shipping line and transit route through the Mediterranean. Container shipping may take 40-50 days.

Are right-hand drive cars allowed in Albania?

No, Albania requires all vehicles to be left-hand drive (LHD). All vehicles from SH GLOBAL are left-hand drive, meeting this requirement.

Is there a vehicle age restriction for imports?

Albania does not have a strict vehicle age limit for imports. However, older vehicles face significantly higher environmental taxes and registration fees. Vehicles meeting Euro 4+ emission standards are recommended to minimize additional costs.

What emission standards do I need to meet?

Albania follows European emission standards. Euro 4 or higher is recommended for imported vehicles. Vehicles with higher Euro standards (Euro 5, Euro 6) benefit from lower environmental fees. Most Korean cars manufactured after 2010 meet Euro 4 requirements.

Can I import a car from Korea as an individual?

Yes, individuals can import vehicles for personal use. SH GLOBAL provides all export documents from Korea. We can also connect you with trusted customs brokers in Durres to handle the local clearance and registration process.

Why buy from Korea instead of Europe?

Korean-market vehicles are typically 20-40% cheaper than equivalent European-sourced cars, with lower mileage and better maintenance records due to Korea's strict inspection system. Mercedes-Benz, BMW, and other European brands sold in Korea are well-maintained and competitively priced.
