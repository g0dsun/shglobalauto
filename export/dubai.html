Why Korean Cars for Dubai

Why Import from Korea to Dubai?

Luxury at a Discount

Korea's fast vehicle turnover cycle means 2-3 year old luxury cars with low mileage enter the used market at significant discounts. A 2024 Mercedes S-Class from Korea can save you 40%+ vs. new GCC price.

Only 5% Customs Duty

UAE's ultra-low 5% import duty makes it one of the cheapest places to import vehicles globally. No VAT on vehicle registration. Jebel Ali Free Zone offers zero-duty for re-exports.

Feature-Rich Specs

Korean-market vehicles come loaded with features that are paid options in GCC spec: HUD, ventilated seats, 360 camera, advanced driver assistance — often standard even on base trims.

Re-Export Hub

Dubai is a global re-export hub. Import premium Korean vehicles to Jebel Ali Free Zone and re-export to Africa, CIS, or other Middle East markets duty-free. SH GLOBAL supports B2B traders.

Importing a Car to Dubai / UAE

Import Duties and Fees

Total effective rate: only ~5% customs duty for personal registration. Zero duty if importing to free zone for re-export.

Key Regulations

  • No strict age limit for personal imports — but vehicles must pass RTA inspection
  • Left-hand drive only — all Korean vehicles comply
  • RTA inspection required for road registration in Dubai
  • Emirates ID and UAE residency required for vehicle registration
  • GCC specifications not mandatory — Korean-spec vehicles can be registered
  • Jebel Ali Free Zone — zero duty for transit/re-export operations

Required Documents

  • Bill of Lading (B/L)
  • Commercial invoice
  • Korean export certificate
  • Vehicle registration copy from Korea
  • Performance inspection report
  • Emirates ID and passport copy (for registration)
  • Certificate of origin

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the customs duty for importing a car to UAE?

Only 5% of the CIF value. This is one of the lowest rates globally. If importing to Jebel Ali Free Zone for re-export, the duty is 0%.

How long does shipping take from Korea to Dubai?

2-3 weeks from Busan to Jebel Ali. Port clearance is typically 2-5 business days. Total delivery time is approximately 3-4 weeks.

Can I register a Korean-spec car in Dubai?

Yes. Korean-spec vehicles can be registered in Dubai after passing RTA inspection. While GCC-spec is preferred by dealers, private imports of Korean-spec vehicles are permitted and common.

Is there a vehicle age limit for UAE imports?

No strict age limit for personal imports. However, older vehicles must pass RTA inspection, and insurance costs increase with age. For commercial resale, newer vehicles (under 5 years) have the best market.

Can I use Dubai as a re-export hub?

Yes. Import vehicles to Jebel Ali Free Zone at 0% duty and re-export to Africa, CIS, or other markets. SH GLOBAL supports B2B traders with volume orders and logistics coordination.
