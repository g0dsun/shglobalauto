Key Regulations

  • 5-year age limit — vehicles must be manufactured within 5 years of the import year
  • Left-hand drive only — Korean vehicles are fully compliant
  • SASO conformity — vehicles must meet Saudi Standards Organization requirements
  • MVPI inspection — Saudi vehicle periodic inspection at Fahas centers
  • Iqama/national ID required for vehicle registration
  • One vehicle per year for personal import (individuals)
  • VIN verification — chassis number must match all documents

Age limit note: In 2026, vehicles manufactured in 2021 or later are eligible for import. SH GLOBAL ensures all vehicles meet Saudi Arabia's age requirement before shipping. We specialize in 1-3 year old premium vehicles — the sweet spot for Saudi imports.

Required Documents

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the vehicle age limit for Saudi Arabia?

Saudi Arabia allows import of vehicles manufactured within the last 5 years. In 2026, vehicles from 2021 or later qualify. SH GLOBAL specializes in recent-model vehicles that meet this requirement.

What is the total import cost?

Total cost = vehicle price + shipping ($900-2,500) + customs (5% duty + 15% VAT = ~20.75% of CIF). For a $30,000 vehicle, expect approximately $39,000-42,000 total landed cost.

How long does shipping take?

To Dammam: 2-3 weeks. To Jeddah: 3-4 weeks. Port clearance in Saudi Arabia typically takes 5-10 business days with proper documentation.

Will a Korean-spec car pass SASO inspection?

Most modern Korean vehicles (2021+) meet SASO requirements. Some minor differences from GCC spec may exist (climate calibration), but these do not typically prevent registration. SH GLOBAL verifies compliance before export.

Can I import more than one car per year?

Individuals can import one vehicle per year for personal use. Commercial importers with proper trade licenses can import multiple vehicles. Contact us about B2B dealer programs.

Is Hyundai/Kia service available in Saudi Arabia?

Yes, extensively. Hyundai (NAGHI Motors) and Kia (Al Jabr Trading) have dealer networks across all major Saudi cities including Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam, Makkah, and Madinah. Parts and service are widely available.

Which port should I ship to — Dammam or Jeddah?

Dammam is ideal for Eastern Province and Riyadh buyers (closer, faster transit). Jeddah serves Western Province buyers. Dammam is generally 1 week faster for shipping from Korea and slightly cheaper.
