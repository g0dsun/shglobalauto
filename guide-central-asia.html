Top Korean Cars for Central Asia — Import Guide

Central Asia has become one of the largest markets for Korean used vehicles. With favorable import policies and high demand for quality cars, countries like Kyrgyzstan, Kazakhstan, and Uzbekistan import thousands of Korean vehicles every year.

Why Central Asia Loves Korean Cars

  • Climate compatibility — Korean cars are built for four-season weather, perfect for Central Asian climates
  • Parts availability — Hyundai and Kia have official service networks across the region
  • Left-hand drive — Korean domestic vehicles are left-hand drive, matching Central Asian roads
  • Competitive pricing — Significantly cheaper than European imports
  • Modern technology — Advanced safety features, fuel efficiency, and comfort
  • Russian-language support — Many Korean cars have Russian-language navigation and infotainment

Country-Specific Import Guide

Kyrgyzstan

  • Import policy: Liberal import rules, one of the easiest Central Asian markets
  • Age limit: No strict limit, but older vehicles face higher customs based on engine size
  • Customs duty: Based on engine displacement (cc) and vehicle age
  • Transit route: Korea → Vladivostok (Russia) → overland to Kyrgyzstan, or Korea → China → Kyrgyzstan
  • Popular models: Hyundai Tucson, Kia Sportage, Genesis G80, BMW X5
  • Market trend: Strong demand for SUVs and sedans in the $10,000-30,000 range

Kazakhstan

  • Import policy: EAEU member — follows Eurasian Economic Union customs rules
  • Age limit: No strict limit, duty varies by age and engine size
  • Customs duty: EAEU unified tariff, calculated per cc of engine displacement
  • Emission standard: Euro-4 minimum required
  • Transit route: Korea → Vladivostok → Trans-Siberian rail/truck → Kazakhstan
  • Popular models: Hyundai Sonata, Genesis GV70, Kia K5, Mercedes-Benz E-Class
  • Market trend: Growing demand for premium and luxury vehicles

Uzbekistan

  • Import policy: Protective tariffs favoring domestic production (GM Uzbekistan/Chevrolet)
  • Age limit: Vehicles must generally be less than 3 years old for reasonable duty rates
  • Customs duty: High — can exceed 100% for older vehicles
  • Key consideration: Best for nearly-new premium vehicles where the duty-to-value ratio is favorable
  • Popular models: Genesis G80/G90, Mercedes-Benz S-Class, BMW 7 Series
  • Market trend: Luxury segment growing rapidly among business buyers

Georgia

  • Import policy: Very liberal, one of the easiest markets globally
  • Age limit: No age restriction
  • Customs duty: Low — 5% for most vehicles + 18% VAT
  • Transit route: Korea → shipping to Poti/Batumi port
  • Popular models: All segments popular, especially SUVs and sedans
  • Market trend: Major re-export hub for the region
